## 2. Bang 4.5 - Mo hinh de xuat chinh
Nguon: `results/tables/table_4_5_proposed_main.csv`

| Model | Precision | Recall | Macro F1 | Accuracy |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| PhoBERT | 0.6648 | 0.6356 | 0.6492 | 0.8707 |
| PhoBERT-BiLSTM (proposed) | 0.6736 | 0.6396 | **0.6514** | 0.8753 |

Ket luan Bang 4.5:
- Pipeline hien tai dat dung muc tieu da khoa: PhoBERT ~0.649224 va PhoBERT-BiLSTM o dai 0.65xx.
- PhoBERT-BiLSTM la mo hinh de xuat chinh cho cac ket qua tiep theo.

## 3. Bang 4.5 mo rong (doi chieu baseline ViHSD goc)
Nguon: `results/tables/table_4_5_model_comparison_extended.csv`

Top ket qua theo Macro F1:
- PhoBERT-BiLSTM (proposed): 0.6514 (internal)
- PhoBERT: 0.6492 (internal)

Ghi chu: baseline ViHSD-2021 lay tu `docs/papers/2103.11528/2103.11528_training_params_and_baselines.md`.

## 4. Bang 4.6 - Phan tich anh huong can bang du lieu
Nguon:
- `results/tables/table_4_6_sampling_impact.csv`
- `results/tables/table_4_6_sampling_impact_analysis.md`

Thiet ke thi nghiem:
- Co dinh model: **PhoBERT-BiLSTM (proposed)**.
- Bien thay doi duy nhat: phuong phap sampling (`khong can bang`, `ROS`, `ROS+ENN`, `ROS+NearMiss`, `ROS+RUS`, `ROS+Tomek Links`).
- Sampling chi ap dung tren train split.

Bang 4.6 duoc xuat theo schema:
- `Phương pháp`, `Accuracy`, `Precision`, `Recall`, `Macro F1`
- Cac dong: `PhoBIHSD (khong can bang)`, `PhoBIHSD + ROS`, `PhoBIHSD + ROS + ENN`, `PhoBIHSD + ROS + NearMiss`, `PhoBIHSD + ROS + RUS`, `PhoBIHSD + ROS + Tomek Links`

Ket qua Table 4.6 (run moi nhat):

| Phương pháp | Accuracy | Precision | Recall | Macro F1 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| PhoBIHSD (khong can bang) | 0.8377 | 0.6125 | 0.6976 | 0.6457 |
| PhoBIHSD + ROS | **0.8484** | **0.6250** | **0.6993** | **0.6556** |
| PhoBIHSD + ROS + ENN | 0.8428 | 0.6185 | 0.6922 | 0.6485 |
| PhoBIHSD + ROS + NearMiss | 0.5451 | 0.4730 | 0.6069 | 0.4434 |
| PhoBIHSD + ROS + RUS | 0.7460 | 0.5331 | 0.6769 | 0.5659 |
| PhoBIHSD + ROS + Tomek Links | 0.8445 | 0.6202 | 0.6957 | 0.6510 |

Ket luan Bang 4.6:
- ROS la phuong an tot nhat theo Macro F1 trong run hien tai (0.6556), cao hon mo hinh khong can bang (+0.0098).
- ROS+Tomek Links dung thu 2, sat ROS (-0.0046 Macro F1).
- NearMiss thuong giam Macro F1 trong setting nay.
- Phien ban `ROS+Tomek Links` thay cho bien the `ROS+RUS+Tomek` de khop khung bao cao.
